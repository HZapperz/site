03The Process

5 stages. 1 dashboard. 0 wondering.

// 01
Sourced
Your parts laid out, photographed with Build #N. You see what's about to be assembled.
// 02
Assembled
Movement seated, dial fitted, hands installed under microscope. Short video of the build.
// 03
Regulated
Movement on the timing machine. Adjusted to ±15 sec/day. The actual readout, photographed.
// 04
QC'd
Pressure tested. Hero photography of the finished watch with your build card.
// 05
Shipped
Tracking, setup video, handwritten note in box. Strap tools included for future swaps.
Every stage hits your email and your dashboard. So you never have to ask “where's my watch?”
