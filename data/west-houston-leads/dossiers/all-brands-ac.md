**Paid-search economics (NW Houston HVAC):** clicks on "ac repair near me" run roughly $8–$25; cost-per-lead commonly **$60–$150**; at a healthy booked-lead close rate, blended **CAC ~$150–$400/job** — easily ROAS-positive on a $700–$1,200 job and dramatically so when it converts into a financed replacement.
