## 7. Vertical Economics & ROI Model

- **Ticket size:** Houston roof replacement averages **~$9,752**, typical range **$6,700–$13,100**, with metal/tile and larger/complex jobs running $20k–$35k+ (VERIFIED, 2025 Houston cost data). Repairs are smaller, but the high-value job is the full re-roof — and storm/insurance jobs (their specialty) skew toward full replacements.
- **Paid-search economics:** Roofing is one of the **most expensive home-service verticals on Google Ads** — CPCs ~$10–$11 nationally, **$40+ per click in Texas/FL/CO**, non-branded CPL commonly **$94–$228** (roofing/gutters topped 2025 at ~$228 CPL), and roofing has one of the **lowest landing-page conversion rates (~3.7%)** of all home services (VERIFIED benchmarks). Branded search is far cheaper (~$44 CPL).
- **Seasonality:** Demand spikes after Gulf Coast storm/hail events (spring–summer) and hurricane season; insurance-claim work is event-driven. Speed-to-lead matters most exactly when volume spikes.
- **ROAS / why conversion lift is huge here:** Because roofing CPLs are so high and the base conversion rate so low (~3.7%), the lever with the best ROI is **not more clicks — it's converting the clicks they already pay for / already earn from referrals.** 

  **Royal-Pawz-style math (illustrative, on THEIR ticket):** Suppose Houston Strong drives 200 qualified site visits/month (organic + referral + any ads). 
  - At a typical ~4% form-only conversion → ~8 leads/month. 
  - Doubling the booked-lead rate to ~8% via instant online booking + Twilio speed-to-lead SMS → ~16 leads/month, i.e. **+8 leads/month.** 
  - At even a conservative 25% close rate on a **$9,752** average job, +8 leads ≈ +2 closed jobs/month ≈ **~$19,500 in added monthly revenue** (~$234k/yr) from the same traffic. 
  - If they ARE buying Google Ads at $150–$200 CPL, halving wasted clicks by fixing the landing page/booking flow is worth thousands per month on its own. 

  The Royal Pawz proof (7% → 30.8% booking conversion, ~5x ROAS) maps almost perfectly: same problem (great service, leaky funnel), and roofing's high ticket means each recovered booking is worth ~$9.7k vs. a grooming appointment — the dollar impact is dramatically larger.
