In Hockley/Cypress/NW Houston, the prospect competes against three tiers:

1. **Aggregator booking platforms — LawnStarter, LawnGuru, GreenPal.** These own the "instant online quote + book in 2 minutes + app rescheduling + card on file" experience. This is *exactly* the frictionless front door Emerald lacks; they out-convert solo operators on speed-to-book.

3. **Other Facebook/Nextdoor owner-operators** — Emerald's true peer set, where the edge goes to whoever has reviews + a bookable link.

**What competitors have that Emerald lacks:** owned website, instant-quote/online booking, retargeting pixels, and visible review counts. **Where Emerald can realistically win:** it already has the craft, local Nextdoor goodwill, job-photo proof, and Jobber operational backbone.
