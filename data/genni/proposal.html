I downloaded Genni and used it. The honest read: the product needs work before a dollar goes to ads.

The underlying AI is built, that's the hard part. But when I actually used it, the core try-on failed, and the experience around it needs a pass. Here's what we fix before we scale spend.

The onboarding

Fix first

The core experience

The marquee feature failed when I used it: I picked a style, tapped generate, and got an error instead of a result (below). If the core try-on doesn't reliably work, nothing downstream matters. Reliability is priority one, with a usability pass right behind it.

The good news: this is the highest-leverage work there is, and it's exactly where the plan starts. Fix the product, then pour fuel on it.

The Opportunity

AI beauty try-on is one of the fastest-rising consumer-AI categories, and the prize is already proven.

The category leader, Perfect Corp (the YouCam apps), books $60M+ a year from roughly one million paying subscribers and 800+ white-label brand partners, and earns about 89% of its revenue from the B2B side.1

They didn't win on AR quality alone. They won on distribution. That's the opening: Genni already ships the consumer app and a B2B "Studio" try-on SDK. The technology is here. Get the experience converting, and distribution is the whole game.

The Competitive Window

Perfect Corp shows the ceiling, but it's a broad, makeup-first incumbent. The AI-native, hairstyle-first niche is still wide open: a long tail of near-identical apps competing on app-store keywords and TikTok before/afters, none with a real moat.2 Moving first and fast on distribution is how Genni takes that niche before the window narrows.

  • Commitment, step by step: each small answer is a micro-yes. By the paywall, subscribing is simply the next yes (the Cal AI / Cialdini playbook).

  • The paywall, engineered: annual-anchored pricing, trial framing, objection-handling, the same hard paywall, A/B tuned on both surfaces and instrumented end to end.

03

Paid acquisition that scales

Meta & TikTok campaigns built on top of the funnel and the creative engine, start lean, find the winning combinations, then scale spend against a clear ROAS target. Plus the organic foundation: ASO and short-form presence to capture trend traffic for free.

Pour in

Concentrate budget on winners; refresh creative before fatigue. Scale only what pays.

  • One number: everything ladders to ROAS / payback, we scale only what's proven to return.
