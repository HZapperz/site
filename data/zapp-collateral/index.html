Printing
  • Open a piece → Cmd-P. Turn ON "Print backgrounds" (More settings) so the cream + amber render.
  • Set scale to 100% and paper to the size noted on each card; don't "fit to page."
  • Tri-folds & flyers are double-sided — print two-sided, flip on the short edge for landscape tri-folds.
  • Business card prints as two card-sized pages (front, back) at bleed size — ideal for a print shop / Moo / Vistaprint upload.
Before the rep uses them
  • Fill the card's [ Rep Name ] / [ Title ] / [ Phone ] placeholders (and a web domain if you have one).
  • Scan the QR with a phone — it should open your Calendly booking page.
