  • Who I am. Hamza, founding engineer at DietAI, where I built the product, the funnel, and the growth systems that carried it to a 7-figure exit. Now one accountable operator instead of an agency plus a dev shop.

03The thesis in plain words

Say it straight: your marketing problem is usually a software problem. When growth stalls, the budget is rarely the real issue — the page, the booking flow, the follow-up, or the data is. An agency can't fix that and a dev shop won't know to. One operator who measures the funnel and writes the code closes the gap between deciding what to do and doing it — and that seam is where revenue leaks.
