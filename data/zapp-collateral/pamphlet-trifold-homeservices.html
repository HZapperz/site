[D] THESIS The thesis

Your marketing problem is usually a software problem.

You don't have a marketing problem and a separate engineering problem. You have one revenue system — and it breaks at the seam between the two.

For a home-service business, that seam is the booking flow. The gaps I see again and again:

  • A "contact form" standing in for real online booking.
  • No instant reply and no after-hours capture.
  • Manual text-back quoting that never scales.
  • No automated review or reminder follow-up.
  • Invisible on paid search where the jobs are.

Most teams split the people who decide what to do from the people who do it. That seam is where revenue leaks. I close it.
